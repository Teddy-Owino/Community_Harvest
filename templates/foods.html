Food Distribution to the Needy

We distribute food to those in need, including school pupils, families, and community members. Below are some of the essential food items we provide.

Blueband

Popular margarine used in households and schools to enrich meals.

Rice

A staple food item, providing essential nutrition to families in need.

Beans

Rich in protein, we provide beans to ensure balanced nutrition.

Sugar

Sweetener for families and schools to complement meals.

Maize Flour

Used to make porridge and staple food for families.

Cooking Oil

Essential for cooking, providing fats for energy and growth.
